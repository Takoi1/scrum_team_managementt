Welcome to Scrum Task Management

Project Topic :

The agile "SCRUM" method is a flexible, reactive and adaptable approach which favors collaboration and dialogue between several people, each in turn is occupied by such a specific task..

Problematic :

The sprint backlog contains several tasks defined by the entire scrum team, in turn the scrum master takes care of assigning tasks to the development team.
For this we are in front of realizing this concept with an application that we offer flexibility in the realization of projects in the future.
